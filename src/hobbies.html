Hobbies

Art,, in its essence, is the purest expression of human emotion and creativity. It speaks to the heart, transcends words, and leaves a piece of the creator in every stroke, note, or story. I believe hobbies, in their subtle ways, are reflections of art. They are the quiet acts of creation, connection, and discovery that shape who we are. As Albert Camus once said, The literal meaning of life is whatever you’re doing that prevents you from killing yourself. To me, hobbies embody this meaning. They are the anchor in the chaos, the spark of joy that keeps us going. Whether it’s getting lost in the world of movies, feeling the rhythm of music, savoring the process of cooking, or finding new perspectives in books, hobbies are more than pastimes—they are lifelines. They remind us of the beauty in living, the art in existing, and the profound desire to keep seeking, dreaming, and creating.
